# 🛡️ Shield Insurance Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze revenue performance, customer behavior, sales channels, and demographic trends for Shield Insurance.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of a Data Analyst Virtual Internship. The objective was to transform business requirements and client mockups into an interactive reporting solution that helps stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor revenue and customer performance
* Track monthly business growth trends
* Analyze sales mode effectiveness
* Understand customer demographics and policy preferences
* Support business decision-making through interactive reporting

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX
* Power Query
* Data Modeling
* Excel

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

| KPI                     | Description                      |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Total Revenue           | Overall revenue generated        |
| Total Customers         | Total customer base              |
| Monthly Revenue Growth  | Month-over-Month revenue change  |
| Monthly Customer Growth | Month-over-Month customer change |
| DRG                     | Daily Revenue Growth             |
| DCG                     | Daily Customer Growth            |

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Features

✅ Interactive Landing Page Navigation

✅ Dynamic KPI Tracking

✅ Month-over-Month Growth Analysis

✅ Customer Segmentation Analysis

✅ Revenue & Customer Distribution Analysis

✅ Sales Channel Performance Tracking

✅ Age Group Analysis

✅ Interactive Filters & Slicers

✅ Business-Focused Dashboard Design
